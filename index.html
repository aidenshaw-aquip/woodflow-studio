Our Story

Dreaming up the piece you've imagined

חולמים על רהיטים ייחודיים ופריטים בעיצוב אישי? אנחנו כאן כדי להגשים את החלום — עבודות עץ ייחודיות בשילוב אפוקסי.

Wood Flow Studio is a small workshop built on one idea: that the things in your home should be unique, personal, and made by hand. We pair character timber with hand-poured epoxy, letting the grain of each slab guide where the resin flows.

From a live-edge river dining table to an olive-wood mezuzah for the doorway, every piece is designed with you and poured to order — so what you receive is genuinely one of a kind.
